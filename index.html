About Us

La Consolacion College is dedicated to providing holistic education that nurtures academic excellence, character development, and service to society. Founded on the principles of St. Augustine and managed by the Augustinian Sisters, our institution has been a beacon of quality education since its establishment.

Academic Excellence

Our rigorous academic programs are designed to challenge students to reach their full potential through critical thinking and innovative learning approaches.

Values Formation

We instill core values of integrity, respect, and compassion, preparing students to become ethical leaders in their communities.

Supportive Community

Our nurturing environment fosters personal growth, creating a sense of belonging where every student feels valued and supported.

Our Programs

La Consolacion College offers a comprehensive range of academic programs designed to prepare students for their future careers and personal development. Our curriculum integrates theoretical knowledge with practical skills, ensuring graduates are ready to face real-world challenges.

Basic Education

Our K-12 program provides a strong foundation for lifelong learning, with a curriculum that balances academics, arts, and physical education.

College Programs

We offer diverse undergraduate programs in Education, Business, Information Technology, Hospitality Management, and more.

Graduate Studies

Advance your career with our specialized master's programs designed for working professionals seeking to enhance their expertise.
